Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how AnangTawiah.com, LLC (“we,” “our,” “us”) collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information when you interact with our website, subscribe to our reports, or use our services. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, so we encourage you to review it periodically. If any material changes are made, we will notify you by posting a prominent notice on our website.

  1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information to provide and improve our services. The types of information we collect include:

  • Contact Information: Your name and email address, collected when you subscribe to our reports or contact us.
  • Identifiers: Your IP address and device identifiers, collected via server logs and security tools.
  • Online Activity Data: Information about your interactions with our website, such as pages viewed and links clicked, collected through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communication Data: The content of communications you send us via email or contact forms.
  • Subscription Data: Your email address and preferences, collected when you subscribe to our reports.
  • Technical Information: Details about your browser and operating system, collected via cookies and log files.
  • User-Generated Content: Feedback or questions you submit to us.
  1. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To deliver the reports and content you request.
  • Improving Our Website: To analyze user interactions and enhance the functionality of our website.
  • Responding to Inquiries: To address your questions and provide support.
  • Communicating Updates: To inform you about updates, new reports, and other content that may interest you.
  • Security: To protect our website and users from fraudulent or malicious activity.
  1. How We Share Your Information

We may share your personal information with third parties in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We work with trusted service providers to help operate our website, manage subscriptions, and analyze data. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your personal information and use it only for the services they provide to us.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a legal summons or to comply with regulatory obligations.
  1. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. These technologies help us:

  • Session Management: Remember your interactions and preferences during a session.
  • Personalization: Store your language and content preferences to tailor your experience.
  • Analytics: Understand how visitors use our website, enabling us to improve its performance and content.
  • Security: Detect and prevent potential threats or fraudulent activity.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. For more information about our use of cookies, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

  1. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. When it is no longer necessary to retain your personal information, we will either delete or anonymize it, unless we are required to retain it for legal, regulatory, or compliance purposes.

If you unsubscribe from our reports, we will retain your email address indefinitely to ensure that no further communications are sent to you.

  1. International Data Transfers

Your personal information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of the European Union (EU), European Economic Area (EEA), and the United Kingdom (UK). We collaborate with third-party partners who handle these data transfers and ensure that appropriate safeguards, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and the UK Addendum, are in place to protect your data.

Please note that we do not store user data or analytics directly. Instead, our third-party partners are responsible for implementing and maintaining these safeguards to ensure that your personal information is protected during transfer and processing. These partners are contractually obligated to comply with the relevant data protection laws and use your information only for the services they provide to us.

  1. Data Security

We implement physical, technical, and administrative measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. These measures include:

  • Encryption: Personal data is encrypted both during transmission and at rest.
  • Access Control: Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only, based on a “need to know” basis.
  • Vendor Assessments: Before sharing personal information with third-party vendors, we assess their security practices to ensure they meet our standards.
  • Employee Training: All employees with access to personal information undergo regular training on data protection and security protocols.

Despite these measures, no security system is entirely foolproof, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

  1. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@anangtawiah.com.

  1. Additional Information for California Residents

California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:

  • The Right to Know: You have the right to request information about the personal data we collect, use, and share about you.
  • The Right to Delete: You may request that we delete personal data we have collected from you, subject to certain exceptions.
  • The Right to Opt-Out of Sale: While we do not sell personal data, you have the right to opt out of any future sale of your personal information.
  • The Right to Non-Discrimination: You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.
  1. Additional Information for EU/EEA/UK Residents

If you are a resident of the EU, EEA, or UK, you may have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  1. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed toward users (i.e. site visitors, subscribers, etc) under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

  1. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at info@anangtawiah.com.
